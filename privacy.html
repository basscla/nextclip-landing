Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

1. Introduction

NextClip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension for YouTube channel monitoring and viral video tracking.

By using NextClip, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • YouTube Channel Data: Channel URLs, channel names, and video information that you choose to bookmark and monitor.
  • API Keys: YouTube API keys and Gemini API keys that you optionally provide for enhanced features.
  • Subscription Information: Your subscription tier (Free, Starter, Professional, or Business) and billing information managed through LemonSqueezy.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, channels monitored, folders created, and interaction patterns within the extension.
  • YouTube Data: Video titles, view counts, upload dates, and channel statistics for channels you monitor.
  • Browser Information: Chrome extension version, browser type, and operating system.

2.3 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Channel bookmarks, folders, settings, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome Storage API.
  • Cloud Storage: User authentication data and subscription information are stored on Supabase (our database provider).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide access to your account.
  • Core Functionality: To enable channel bookmarking, video monitoring, and analytics features.
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription tier and enforce feature limits.
  • YouTube Integration: To fetch channel data, video statistics, and monitor new uploads using YouTube Data API.
  • Notifications: To send alerts about new videos from monitored channels (with your permission).
  • Data Export: To allow you to export your data to Excel, CSV, or Google Sheets.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our features (anonymized data only).
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

4.1 Third-Party Services We Use

  • Google APIs:
    • YouTube Data API v3 - For fetching channel and video information
    • Google OAuth 2.0 - For user authentication
    • Google Sheets API - For data export functionality
    • Google Gemini API - For AI-powered analytics (optional)
  • Supabase: Our database provider for storing user accounts and subscription data. Data is stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure.
  • LemonSqueezy: Our payment processor for handling subscriptions. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Telegram API: For sending notifications (only if you enable this feature and provide your Telegram bot token).

4.2 Data Not Shared

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Access your private YouTube data beyond what you explicitly authorize

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Important: NextClip's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We use Google OAuth and YouTube Data API to:

  • Authenticate users via Google Sign-In
  • Access public YouTube channel data for channels you choose to monitor
  • Fetch video statistics and channel information
  • Export data to Google Sheets (with your explicit permission)

Limited Use Disclosure: NextClip's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use the minimum permissions necessary for our core functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access Control: User data is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies in Supabase.
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 secure authentication via Google.
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data like API keys are stored locally in your browser, not on our servers.
  • API Keys: We never transmit your API keys to our servers; they are used only within your browser.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by NextClip through the extension settings.
  • Export: Download your data in CSV or Excel format at any time.
  • Delete: Remove individual channels, folders, or all your data from the extension.
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your Google account from NextClip at any time.

7.2 Data Retention

  • Active Users: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Inactive Accounts: If you don't use NextClip for 12 months, we may delete your account data.
  • Cancelled Subscriptions: After cancellation, we retain subscription data for 30 days for reactivation, then delete it.
  • Local Data: Data stored in your browser remains until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.

7.3 Opting Out

  • Notifications: Disable browser notifications in extension settings or Chrome settings.
  • Telegram Notifications: Remove your Telegram bot token from settings.
  • Data Collection: Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection.

8. Children's Privacy

NextClip is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country. By using NextClip, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

Our data is primarily stored on:

  • Supabase infrastructure (AWS regions)
  • Your local browser storage

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing a notification in the extension (for material changes)

Your continued use of NextClip after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Cookies and Tracking

NextClip does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts. All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API or in our secure database.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about the data we collect.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information).
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and for contract fulfillment.
  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time.

  • Email: privacy@nextclip.io

Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2024

1. Introduction

NextClip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name (optional)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Paddle.com (we do not store credit card details)
  • YouTube Channels: Channel IDs and names you choose to monitor
  • Preferences: Settings and configurations within the extension

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features used, frequency of use, performance metrics
  • Device Information: Browser version, operating system
  • YouTube Data: Public channel statistics and video information via YouTube API

3. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Provide monitoring services YouTube channel IDs, user preferences
Process payments Email, payment details (via Paddle)
Send notifications Email, Telegram ID (if provided)
Improve services Usage analytics, error reports
Customer support Email, account information

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored using Chrome's synchronized storage API
  • Local storage is used for temporary caching
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • Payment processing is handled by Paddle.com with industry-standard encryption

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Paddle for payment processing, Telegram for notifications (if enabled)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing

6. YouTube API Services

NextClip uses YouTube API Services. By using our extension, you also agree to:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

You can revoke NextClip's access to your YouTube data via Google Security Settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

NextClip is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The NextClip extension itself does not use cookies. However, our website may use cookies for analytics and functionality. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

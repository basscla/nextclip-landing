Refund Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2024

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied with NextClip, contact us within 7 days of your initial purchase for a full refund.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

1.1 Eligible for Refund:

  • First-time subscribers within 7 days of initial purchase
  • Service outages lasting more than 48 consecutive hours
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Technical issues that prevent core functionality despite support efforts

1.2 Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Requests made after 7 days from initial purchase
  • Renewal charges (after the first billing cycle)
  • Partial month usage
  • Violations of our Terms of Service
  • Changes in YouTube API or third-party services

2. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at support@nextclip.app within 7 days of purchase
  2. Include your order number and email associated with the purchase
  3. Briefly explain the reason for your refund request
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours

3. Refund Processing

  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Refund Method: Original payment method
  • Currency: Original payment currency
  • Notification: Email confirmation when processed

4. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges:

  • Cancel through the NextClip extension settings
  • Cancel through Paddle's customer portal
  • Contact support for assistance

Note: Cancellation stops future charges but doesn't automatically trigger a refund. You retain access until the end of your current billing period.

5. Prorated Refunds

We generally do not offer prorated refunds for partial month usage. However, exceptions may be made for:

  • Extended service outages
  • Account termination due to our error
  • Special circumstances at our discretion

6. Free Trial and Free Tier

  • Free tier users are not eligible for refunds (no payment made)
  • Upgrading from free tier starts the 7-day guarantee period
  • Downgrading doesn't qualify for refunds of previous charges

7. Annual Plans

Annual subscriptions follow the same 7-day money-back guarantee. After 7 days:

  • No refunds for remaining months
  • You can cancel to prevent renewal
  • Access continues until the end of the annual period

8. Disputes and Chargebacks

We encourage customers to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback. Chargebacks may result in:

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Loss of data and settings
  • Inability to use the service in the future

9. Special Circumstances

We may offer refunds outside this policy for:

  • Medical emergencies
  • Natural disasters affecting service access
  • Other exceptional circumstances at our discretion

10. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

Email: support@nextclip.app
Response Time: Within 48 hours
Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM KST

11. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

Customer Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is important to us. If you're experiencing issues, please contact our support team before requesting a refund. We're often able to resolve problems quickly.

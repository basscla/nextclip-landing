Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NextClip

What is NextClip and how does it work? ‚ñº
NextClip is a Chrome extension that helps you monitor multiple YouTube channels from a convenient side panel. It tracks channel statistics, sends notifications for new videos, and provides analytics to help you understand channel performance trends.
How often does NextClip update channel data? ‚ñº
Both new video detection and viral video rankings update every 15 minutes for all plans. Ranking notifications are sent 3 times daily (9 AM, 3 PM, 9 PM) to keep you informed of viral trends without overwhelming your inbox. You can also check rankings anytime directly in the extension.
Can I export my data from NextClip? ‚ñº
Yes! All paid plans include comprehensive data export functionality. You can export your bookmarked channels, 30 days of discovered new videos, and all analytics data in CSV or JSON format. Even better, you can import these exported files back into NextClip anytime to restore your settings and data. Professional and Enterprise plans also include API access for programmatic data retrieval.
Is my data secure with NextClip? ‚ñº
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data transmission and storage. NextClip is GDPR compliant, and we never share your data with third parties. We use OAuth 2.0 for YouTube authentication, so we never store your passwords.
Can I try NextClip before purchasing? ‚ñº
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start. Additionally, all paid plans come with a 14-day money-back guarantee.

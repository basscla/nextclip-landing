Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn?

Starting at 25% commission, you can earn $2.50-20 per sale depending on the plan. As you reach higher tiers (up to 40%), you'll earn $4-32 per sale! Plus, it's recurring - earn every single month for as long as your referrals stay subscribed!

When do I get paid?

Payouts are processed monthly on NET30 terms. For example, commissions earned in January are paid out on March 15th. Minimum payout is $50.

How do tier upgrades work?

Completely automatic! Our system checks your sales daily. When you hit 11 sales, you jump to 30%. At 31 sales, you reach 35%. At 76+ sales, you hit the maximum 40%. You'll get an email notification when you're upgraded, and the new rate applies to ALL future sales automatically.

Is the commission really lifetime?

Yes! You earn recurring commission for every single month that your referral stays subscribed. There's no limit - if they stay for 5 years, you earn for 5 years. This is true passive income at whatever tier you've reached!

What is the cookie duration?

Our cookies last 90 days. If someone clicks your link and subscribes within 90 days, you get credit for the sale.

Do I need to be approved?

Most affiliates are approved automatically. We review applications to ensure quality and prevent spam, but honest promoters are always welcome.

Can I promote via paid ads?

Yes! You can use Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other platforms. However, trademark bidding (bidding on "NextClip" keyword) is not allowed.

Where can I see my stats?

You can track your clicks, conversions, and earnings in real-time through our Affiliate Dashboard (just login with your Google account), or in the NextClip extension Pricing tab.

What marketing materials do you provide?

We provide banners, social media graphics, email templates, and pre-written copy. You'll also get early access to product updates and exclusive content to share.
